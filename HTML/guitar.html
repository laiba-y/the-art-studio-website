About Our Guitar Classes

Our guitar classes combine hands-on practice, song-based learning and gentle music theory. Whether you want to play acoustic ballads, electric riffs, or fingerstyle pieces, we offer friendly instruction for all ages and levels. Expect small groups, supportive teachers and a welcoming studio environment.

Students learn posture, chords, strumming patterns, basic music notation, and how to play full songs. We offer loaner instruments for beginners and free practice corners during open hours.
